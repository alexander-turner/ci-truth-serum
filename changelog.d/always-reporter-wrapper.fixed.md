- `check-always-reporter`, `check-static-concurrency`, and `check-required-reporter` now recognize a `${{ always() }}`-wrapped reporter `if:` as equivalent to bare `always()`. Previously the exact-string match missed the wrapper, so a gated workflow whose reporter used `if: ${{ always() }}` escaped all three checks (false negative). A compound `always() && <cond>` is still intentionally not a reporter.
